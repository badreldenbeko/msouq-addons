Key Features

Go to elearning module, select courses menu item, under it you will find a courses sub menu.


Click on basics of gardening, for the sake of testing this module.


Click on edit.


Select "Add Content" in the list view under content.


A wizard will open, let's select type of content. Select the dropdown arrow there.


In our case the type of content is "Video".


Now, let's upload our video file.


Here's mine, we have to give title as well to our content.


Here in the list view we got our content as well.


Let's save the form view.
